What Can I Cook

Privacy Policy

Last updated March 13, 2026

This policy explains what data What Can I Cook stores locally on your device and what advertising data may be handled by third-party ad services used in the app.

What this app stores

What Can I Cook stores saved meals, favorite ingredients, recent ingredients, and your last-used filters on your device so the app opens faster and feels more personal.

Ads and third parties

The app uses Google AdMob to show ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers, approximate location, diagnostic data, and ad interaction data under Google's own privacy practices.

How data is used

Stored meal and ingredient data is used only to power search, saved meals, recent items, favorites, filters, and meal suggestions inside the app.

Your choices

You can clear saved choices inside the app at any time by removing saved meals, clearing recent ingredients, or uninstalling the app to remove local data.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed through the project repository at github.com/DenseWatermelon/what-to-cook.

If GitHub Pages is enabled for the repository using the docs/ folder on the main branch, this page will be available at densewatermelon.github.io/what-to-cook/privacy-policy.html.
